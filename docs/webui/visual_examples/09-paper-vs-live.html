Paper Engine — Simulation Concept
Architecture Phase
📊
Virtual Order Book
Internal matching engine processes signals as if sent to broker. Fills simulated at current bid/ask with configurable slippage model.
💰
Shadow Account
Parallel balance, equity, and margin tracking. Mirrors real account structure without touching broker funds. Full PnL attribution per desk.
📡
Tagged SSE Stream
Paper events flow through same SSE channel, tagged [PAPER]. UI can filter or overlay paper vs live events side-by-side.
Mode Transition — Impact Checklist
Governance Rules Planned
L
Live → Paper: Open Positions
Existing live positions remain with broker. Paper engine does not inherit live state. Operator must close or leave running.
P
Paper → Live: Confirmation Gate
Switching to live requires explicit operator confirmation. No automatic escalation from paper to live execution.
!
Mixed Mode Warning
Running LIVE account + PAPER execution means signals are generated from real data but not executed. Drift between paper and actual account is expected.
!
Risk Budget Isolation
Paper mode has its own risk budget. Live risk limits are not consumed by paper trades. No cross-mode budget contamination.
L
Custody Engine Scope
In paper mode, custody actions (trail, hold, close) apply only to simulated positions. Live positions are unaffected by paper custody decisions.
P
Logging & Audit
All paper executions are logged separately with [PAPER] prefix. Audit trail clearly distinguishes simulated from real activity.
!
Demo Account Note
DEMO + LIVE mode sends real orders to a demo account. Useful for integration testing with real MT5 flow but no capital risk.
!
Transition Lock
Mode switch locks out for 30s after any execution to prevent mid-trade mode confusion. Cooldown enforced at control-plane level.
